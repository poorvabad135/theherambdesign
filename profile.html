The Story

From passion to a full creative studio

Founded in 1998 by Professional Artist, Prashant Sudhakar Badgujar, Heramb began with a passion for creativity. Today, that artistic vision grows into full creative studio that serves design, branding, printing, photography and immersive visual experiences. We transform your ideas into impactful experiences which makes the Heramb true to its tag "Dreams come true".

1998

The Beginning

Heramb Designing & Printing opens its doors, starting with core printing services for the local community.

2005–2012

Expanding Our Creative Expertise

Creative and graphic design services are added, followed by package design as more local businesses ask for branded packaging.

2013–2019

Becoming A Complete Creative Partner

Event photography and stall management join the service list, turning Heramb into a one-stop studio for brands and events alike.

Today

1000+ Customers Strong

Heramb now serves individuals, shops, manufacturers and event organisers across the region — still hands-on, still personal.
